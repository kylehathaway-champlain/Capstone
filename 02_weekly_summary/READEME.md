# Weekly Progress Summaries

## Purpose

This directory serves as the log of the Capstone project. Each summary tracks progress, documents decisions and challenges faced.


## Standard Summary Template

Each weekly file must adhere to the following structure:

### 1. Introductory Information: Report Title, Your Name, Organization Name, Contributing Personnel.

### 2. Table of Contents:Provide page numbers to all major and significant minor report sections.

### 3. Introduction: A brief description of what your Project Topic is about and what "Product" will be created from your work.

### 4. Timeline: Proposed for work to be completed!

### 5. Main Report Sections:
* Summary of the work completed during this reporting period.
* Digital Evidence of work completed during this reporting period.

### 6. Conclusions: Provide a unique conclusion (each report) that is a self reflection of your efforts over the reporting period. Conclusions evaluate your accomplishments over the reporting period versus your milestones presented in your week timeline.

### 7. Bibliography: All statements of fact, conclusions, and assertions not your own original work or ideas must be correctly referenced in the text and cited in the Bibliography or in footnotes throughout the document.
